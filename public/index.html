Seriously Kyle! Thoughts


5000 randomly generated images of Kyle, an original creation, with rarity traits.

Seriously Kyle! was created as a way to show through art the many mental and emotional states a teenager in todays world may go through. The two versions of Seriously Kyle are currently the colorful and unique expressions of mental states that are currently available and this more recent version of Kyle and his moments of thought.

We look forward to future collections of Kyle and his thoughts on topics ranging from medicine and law to pondering life's big questions "Why is there even a crunchy peanut butter?", Seriously Kyle!
